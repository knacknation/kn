Finance and Logistics

  1. Home
  2. About
  3. Wings

Head: S.M. Nazmus Shahdat

Finance and Logistics are closely related to each other to function properly for its activities. It is very crucial for a club to prepare a financial budget for its upcoming events, competitions, and to be held sessions. Furthermore, the logistics segment of the wing is held responsible for how to get things done by the club executives and dealing with people. This wing requires managerial skills and a good & clear decorating sense among the executives. The focus point of logistics is “Getting Things Done”.

  • The wing is responsible for preparing a reasonable budget taking all considerations such as how and from where sufficient amount of funds will be collected and how the money will be distributed in each task and wing for proper utilization of the fund collected.

  • The wing ensures proper money maintenance among the other wings so that all other wings can perform efficiently.

  • Logistics segment of the wing is held responsible for how to get things done by the club executives and dealing with people.

  • Logistics segment also manages program and events covering top to bottom management including getting required supplies and materials for events or competitions, getting necessary materials for decoration of the venue, and above managing all the people working under the wing.
