Recruitment

  1. Home
  2. Join Us
  3. Recruitment

Due to the recent pandemic, we conducted ExecQuest 2020,recruitment process of Knack Nation, online. This recruitment process consisted of three rounds.

1st Round: Online Application. Under the regular application section, applicants had to answer some questions related to individual and situational analysis. Individuals who knew the basics in at least one of these fields- Graphic designing, Video editing, Web development, and Content writing were eligible to apply in Special Skill section.



2nd Round: Task Submission. In this round, the general applicants who had qualified in the first round were divided into some groups monitored by moderators set by the club and they had to prepare the solution in group. Besides, special skill applicants were given specific tasks according to their field.

Final Round: Online Interview. Finally, applicants who qualified in the second round had to face an interview arranged by the club.

After being assessed rigorously through these three rounds, selected applicants were recruited in the club.
