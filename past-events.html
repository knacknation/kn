SLIDE FIESTA 2016

Event Information

  • Type: Competition
  • Date: August 15-25, 2016
  • Event Page: Slide Fiesta: A Slide Making Competiton

“Slide Fiesta 2016- A Slide Making Competition”, the first formal competition organized by Knack Nation was the pioneering opportunity seen in FBS to develop skills on PowerPoint. The participants had to go through two rounds for the competition. The candidates were given a theme on which they had to prepare fifteen slides within due time. Candidates selected from the first round were given a dense and critical theme in the second round on which ten slides were to be prepared within shorter time limit and then present the slides in front of the judges.
