Head: Suhala Tahsin Nirjhar


“I am convinced that nothing we do is more important than hiring and developing people. At the end of the day, you bet on people, not on strategies.” - Lawrence Bossidy. Mr. Lawrence, from his 30 years’ experience, said that employees are the most valuable asset for an organization. And Knack Nation is not a variation either. Diligent and energetic presidium members, collaborators, and executives are the core heart of Knack Nation. Since the inception of its journey, members are the secret to KN’s success. And the human resources wing takes care of these amazing personalities.

  • The human resources wing works with the foundational purpose of enrolling, screening, examining, and allocating the newly recruited executives in the right-wing as per their desire and competence.

  • The wing further monitors other wing’s executives and simultaneously motivate and push them to bring out their best outcome and ultimately evaluate their overall performance and engagement in the club.

  • The wing is also responsible for the promotion of wing executives and further assigns these individuals where he/she suits best according to their talent and interest.
